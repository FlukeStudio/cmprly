## What Makes Brevo Different

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) made one architectural choice that separates it from every major competitor: **you pay for emails sent, not contacts stored.**

This is the pricing equivalent of paying for gas instead of paying for seats in the car, regardless of how many people are in it. For a solopreneur with 50,000 contacts who emails twice a month, Brevo costs $18/month. That same list on Kit costs $199/month. On Mailchimp, $210/month. **Over a year, the difference is approximately $2,200.**

The tradeoff is equally important: Brevo is not built around the creator workflow. There is no digital product selling, no paid newsletter subscriptions, and no creator recommendation network. It's a general-purpose marketing platform that happens to be affordable—not a tool purpose-built for the creator economy.

## Free Plan: The Real Details

**As of July 2026, Brevo's free plan includes:**
- Unlimited contacts (yes, truly unlimited)
- 300 emails per day (~9,000/month with consistent sending)
- Multi-step automation workflows
- 40+ email templates
- Brevo branding on outgoing emails

The daily cap is the only real limitation. For a weekly newsletter to 2,000 subscribers, 300 emails/day is sufficient (you just spread the send across a single day). For anything larger or more frequent, you'll need the Starter plan.

## Automation: Solid, Not Spectacular

Brevo's automation builder supports multi-step workflows with triggers, conditions, and actions—more than adequate for welcome sequences, re-engagement campaigns, and abandoned cart flows.

Compared to Kit's visual automation builder, Brevo's is functional but less polished. You can accomplish the same outcomes, but the experience is closer to filling out forms than designing on a canvas. For a solopreneur who uses 3–5 core automation sequences (welcome, nurture, re-engagement), Brevo delivers 100% of what's needed.

**Where Brevo excels: multi-channel automation.** The same workflow builder can trigger email, SMS, WhatsApp, and transactional messages—a capability that requires separate platforms or premium tiers on competitors.

## Deliverability: The Tradeoff

Brevo uses shared IP pools on its free and Starter plans. This means your deliverability is partially influenced by other senders on the same infrastructure. In independent benchmarks (EmailToolTester 2025), Brevo achieved approximately 88% inbox placement, compared to Kit's 91–99.8%.

**Mitigation:** Upgrade to the Business plan ($18/month) for dedicated IP options. Warm up your domain gradually. Use Brevo's built-in list hygiene tools to remove inactive subscribers. For most solopreneurs, the 88% rate is workable—the difference between 88% and 92% on a 2,500-subscriber list translates to roughly 100 undelivered emails per campaign.

## Who Should Use Brevo

**✅ Brevo is right for you if:**
- Budget is your #1 concern and your list is growing
- You email infrequently (twice a month) to a large list
- You want SMS or WhatsApp marketing alongside email
- You're EU-based and need GDPR-compliant hosting
- You send transactional emails and want them in one platform

**❌ Brevo may not be right if:**
- You're a course creator who wants to sell digital products via email
- You want the most intuitive, creator-first experience (Kit)
- You need 100+ polished, design-rich email templates
- You're building a newsletter as your primary product (Beehiiv)

## FAQ

### Is Brevo really unlimited contacts?
**Yes.** On the free plan, you can store unlimited contacts at no cost. The limit is 300 emails sent per day. On paid plans, you pay per email volume, not per contact stored.

### How does Brevo compare to Kit for automation?
Kit's visual builder is more intuitive and creator-focused. Brevo's is functional but more form-based. For 3–5 core automation sequences, both deliver—the difference is user experience, not capability.

### Does Brevo have an affiliate program?
**Yes.** Through PartnerStack, Brevo offers approximately $50 credit per referral. However, this is significantly less valuable than Kit's 30% lifetime recurring commission for affiliate marketers.

### Can I migrate from Kit or Mailchimp to Brevo?
Yes. Brevo provides import tools for contacts and templates. Expect a learning curve during the switch, and test deliverability carefully during the first month.
